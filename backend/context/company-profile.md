# TaskFlow - Company Profile

## Company Overview
**Name:** TaskFlow Inc.
**Founded:** 2021
**Headquarters:** Austin, Texas
**Industry:** SaaS - Project Management & Collaboration
**Stage:** Series A (Growing rapidly, 2,500+ customers)

## Product
TaskFlow is a modern project management platform designed for distributed teams. We help companies organize work, track progress, and collaborate seamlessly across time zones.

## Core Features
- **Task Management:** Create, assign, and track tasks with custom workflows
- **Team Collaboration:** Real-time comments, @mentions, file attachments
- **Timeline Views:** Gantt charts, Kanban boards, Calendar views
- **Integrations:** Slack, Google Calendar, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, Jira
- **Automation:** Custom rules and triggers to automate repetitive work
- **Reporting:** Analytics dashboards, burndown charts, team velocity metrics
- **Mobile Apps:** iOS and Android native applications

## Pricing Tiers
- **Free:** Up to 5 users, basic features
- **Pro:** $12/user/month - Advanced features, unlimited projects
- **Business:** $24/user/month - Priority support, advanced integrations, SSO
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing - Dedicated support, custom contracts, SLA

## Customer Base
- 2,500+ companies worldwide
- Primary segments: Tech startups (40%), Marketing agencies (25%), Consulting firms (20%), Other (15%)
- Team sizes: 5-50 employees (sweet spot)
- Geographic distribution: North America (60%), Europe (25%), Asia-Pacific (15%)

1. **Integration Problems:** Slack/Google Calendar sync issues (30% of tickets)
2. **Account/Billing:** Subscription changes, invoice requests (20%)
3. **Feature Questions:** "How do I...?" type queries (25%)
4. **Bug Reports:** UI glitches, mobile app issues (15%)
5. **Onboarding Help:** New users needing guidance (10%)

## Business Context
TaskFlow is experiencing 15% month-over-month growth. Support team (currently 3 FTEs) is overwhelmed:
- Average response time: 8 hours (missing SLA)
- Ticket backlog: 200+ open tickets
- Customer satisfaction: 72% (down from 85% six months ago)
- Support costs: $225,000/year (3 FTEs × $75k)

**Goal:** Deploy AI Customer Success Agent to handle 70% of routine inquiries, reduce response time to <1 hour, improve CSAT to 90%, reduce costs to <$1,000/year for AI operations.
